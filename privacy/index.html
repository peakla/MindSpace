Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At MindBalance, we take your privacy seriously. As a student-built educational project, we are committed to being transparent about how we handle information. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Information We Collect

MindBalance collects minimal information necessary to provide our services:

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication only)
  • Display name (optional, for community features)
  • Profile information you choose to share

Usage Data

We may collect basic usage data to improve the website:

  • Pages visited
  • Features used
  • Browser type and device information

How We Use Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Enable community features like posts and comments
  • Improve the website experience
  • Send important service updates (if you opt in)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider with industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

We do not:
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share your data with marketers
  • Store sensitive health information

Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage (similar to cookies) to:

  • Remember your accessibility preferences (dark mode, font size, etc.)
  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Save your reading progress in articles
  • Remember your language preference

These are functional cookies that improve your experience and do not track you across other websites.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - For user authentication and database
  • ElevenLabs - For text-to-speech features (no personal data shared)
  • Google Fonts - For typography

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at mindbalancehelp@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

MindBalance is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mindbalancehelp@gmail.com.
