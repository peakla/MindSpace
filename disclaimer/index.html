About This Website

MindBalance was created by students as an educational project for a web design competition. While this is a competition entry, we have taken great care to ensure that all information presented is factual, accurate, and sourced from credible organizations.

Credible Sources

All mental health information on MindBalance is sourced from reputable and authoritative organizations, including:

  • National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) - A federal agency for research on mental disorders
  • Mayo Clinic - A nonprofit academic medical center
  • American Psychological Association (APA) - The leading scientific and professional organization representing psychology
  • World Health Organization (WHO) - The United Nations agency for international public health
  • SAMHSA - Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration

Each article and resource on our website includes citations and links to the original sources for verification.

Your Data & Privacy

We want to be clear about your data:

  • We do NOT sell your personal information
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT use your data for commercial purposes
  • We only store the minimum information needed for website functionality

For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Not a Substitute for Professional Help

The information on MindBalance is provided for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Important: If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or emergency, please contact emergency services (911) or a crisis helpline immediately. Visit our Support page for crisis resources.

Accuracy of Information

We strive to keep all information accurate and up-to-date. However, mental health research is constantly evolving. While we make every effort to ensure accuracy:

  • Information may become outdated as new research emerges
  • Individual experiences with mental health vary widely
  • Treatments and recommendations may differ based on individual circumstances

We encourage you to verify information with healthcare professionals and consult the original sources linked in our content.
